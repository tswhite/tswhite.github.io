Practice Areas

Facing allegations of criminal misconduct can be intimidating, and present complex legal issues about which you may not even be aware. In many cases, the State may not be able to prove its allegations against you. Missing witnesses, unrecovered evidence, poor police investigations, Constitutional violations, and improper lab maintenance are just a small sample in the pool of reasons why this may be true. The State most likely will not simply dismiss these cases, but attempt to force you into plea negotiations that seem favorable until you are strapped with suspensions, fees, intrusive tests, and burdensome classes.

Our goal is to protect you against such practices. We are proven to be results-oriented in both negotiations and trial. We provide exemplary legal advice on a wide range of criminal law areas, which include but are not limited to the following:
